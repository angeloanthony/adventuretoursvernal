Essential Items

Required for All Tours

  • Valid Driver's License - Required for all drivers (original, not a copy)
  • Closed-Toe Shoes - Sturdy shoes or boots (no sandals, flip-flops, or open-toed shoes)
  • Weather-Appropriate Clothing - See seasonal guide below
⚠️ Important: You will not be permitted to drive without a valid driver's license, and you cannot ride with open-toed shoes. No exceptions for safety reasons.

Seasonal Clothing Guide

🌸 Spring (March - May)

40°F - 70°F

Recommended:

  • Long pants (jeans work great)
  • Long-sleeve shirt or layers
  • Light jacket or windbreaker
  • Closed-toe shoes or boots

Tip: Mornings can be chilly, afternoons warm up. Dress in layers!

☀️ Summer (June - August)

60°F - 95°F

Recommended:

  • Light, breathable long pants
  • Long-sleeve shirt (protects from sun)
  • Bandana or neck gaiter for dust
  • Sturdy closed-toe shoes
  • Extra sunscreen and water

Tip: Long sleeves protect from sun and dust. Bring extra water!

🍂 Fall (September - November)

30°F - 70°F

Recommended:

  • Long pants (jeans or hiking pants)
  • Layers - base layer, mid layer, jacket
  • Gloves (for cooler mornings)
  • Warm closed-toe shoes or boots

Tip: Beautiful fall colors! Bring your camera and extra layers.

❄️ Winter (December - February)

20°F - 50°F

Recommended:

  • Warm, insulated pants
  • Multiple layers on top
  • Warm winter jacket
  • Gloves and warm hat
  • Insulated boots

Tip: Dress warmer than you think! Wind chill on the trail is real.

💼

Optional But Useful

  • Small Backpack or Fanny Pack - For personal items (must be securely closed)
  • Bug Spray - Especially in warmer months near water
  • Hand Wipes/Sanitizer - Gets dusty out there!
  • Chapstick - Desert air is dry
  • Personal Snacks - We provide snacks, but bring extras if you have dietary needs
  • GoPro/Action Camera - Great for hands-free video
  • Bandana or Buff - Dust protection for face
  • Cash/Card - For tips (if you want to tip your guide)

Do NOT Bring

These items are prohibited on our tours:
  • Alcohol or Drugs - Absolutely prohibited, zero tolerance
  • Weapons - No firearms, knives (except small pocket knife), or weapons
  • Glass Containers - Safety hazard on rough terrain
  • Excessive Luggage - Limited storage space available
  • Valuables - Leave expensive jewelry at home
  • Pets - Sorry, no animals allowed for safety reasons

Pro Packing Tips

1
Dress in Layers

Temperature can vary 20-30 degrees throughout the day. Start with a base layer and add/remove as needed.

2
Secure Your Items

Use zippered pockets or a secure bag. Dusty trails can shake things loose!

3
Protect Your Phone/Camera

Dust is everywhere! Use a ziplock bag or protective case.

4
Better to Have It

When in doubt, bring it. Better to have a jacket and not need it than need it and not have it!

5
Leave Valuables Behind

We're going off-road in dusty conditions. Leave expensive items at your hotel.
